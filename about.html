About PakVista Realty

Building trust and delivering excellence in Pakistan's real estate market since 2010.

Our Journey

Transforming Dreams into Addresses Since 2010

PakVista Realty was founded in 2010 with a simple yet powerful vision: to make property transactions transparent, trustworthy, and accessible for everyone across Pakistan. What started as a small office in Islamabad with just three passionate real estate professionals has grown into one of the region's most respected property agencies.

Over the past 14 years, we've helped more than 950 families find their dream homes and assisted countless businesses in securing the perfect commercial spaces. Our success is built on the foundation of integrity, market expertise, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' satisfaction.

Today, PakVista Realty operates across Pakistan's major cities—Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Karachi, and Lahore—with a dedicated team of over 25 expert agents who bring local knowledge, professional guidance, and personalized service to every transaction.
