Frequently Asked Questions

What are your service charges?

Our commission is 1-2% of the property value for buyers and sellers, which is competitive in the Pakistani market. We provide transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

Do you assist with property documentation?

Yes, we provide comprehensive support including title verification, transfer documentation, and can connect you with trusted legal advisors for complete peace of mind.

Can you help with bank financing?

Absolutely! We work with all major banks in Pakistan and can assist you in securing the best mortgage rates and processing your loan application efficiently.

Do you offer property management?

Yes, we provide property management services for landlords including tenant screening, rent collection, maintenance coordination, and regular property inspections.

Which cities do you operate in?

We primarily serve Islamabad, Rawalpindi, Karachi, and Lahore, but our network extends to other major cities across Pakistan for specialized property searches.

How quickly can you find a property?

Depending on your requirements, we typically present suitable options within 3-5 days. Our extensive database and market network ensure quick matches for most needs.
